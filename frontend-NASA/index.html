What challanges do Astronauts face daily

Astronauts have a very challanging job. they spend many months away from their famillies and friends in the hope of advancing our understanding of space and the impacts of zero gravity has on peoples lives.

Some of these challanges include:

  • Lack of physical exercise causing muscle and bone deteroration
  • Overwork with 16 hour shifts and everyday being on call
  • Lack of social connection leading to depression
  • Home sickness / Real sickness

From what we found so far the games on the international space station are ones that have no real objective or competitive aspect that solves all the issues that that are present in the list of problems.

How Does Astro Ball Solve These Issues

Astro Ball aims the use the microgravity enviroment to create a dynamic game that is simple to understand and solve many issues that astronaughts face. the premise is for two opposing players to throw the ball towards the other players direction but no directly at them.

  • Astro Ball requires the users move around the room they in order to catch the ball requiring physical movement
  • Astro Ball creates friendly competition between the players which creates social interaction
  • Astro Ball is not very brain intesive allowing the players to unwind for a little
  • Astro Ball is not a long game so it fits in the 1 hour time limit
  • Astro Ball is similar to games down on earth reliving home sickness

Sometimes less is more. astronaughts are already busy enough. The ideal game should be simple and fun.

The Rules

No game is complete without a set of rules that bind the players into a controlled enviroment. Forcing them to come up with a creative solutions to win and providing ligitimacy to the game.

  • players must be as at other ends of the walls
  • players must throw the ball towards the direction of the other player
  • when players catch the ball they will get a point
  • when players don't catch the ball they will lose a point
  • when the timer goes to zero all players must stop
  • The player with the most points win
